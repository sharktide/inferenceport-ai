You can save useful AI websites to open them quickly from the app. Saved sites appear in the marketplace under 'Websites'.

How it works

  • Save a website from the Marketplace or from its page.
  • Launch opens the site in a focused Chromium window.
  • Delete removes the saved bookmark locally.

Notes

  • Some sites prevent embedding; the app will open them in a new window to ensure full functionality.
  • Saves are local to your machine and do not sync unless you export/import them manually.
