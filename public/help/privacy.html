InferencePortAI is designed for local-first usage. By default chat data and models are stored on your machine and not uploaded to external servers.

Local storage

  • Chat sessions and favorites are stored in your app data folder.
  • Models you pull are stored on disk; deleting a model removes its files.

Network services

  • Hugging Face Spaces and external websites are online services and may collect data according to their policies.
  • The app does not phone home or send your conversations anywhere unless you explicitly use a share feature.
