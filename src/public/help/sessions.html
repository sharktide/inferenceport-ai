The app stores chat sessions locally so you can continue conversations later. Sessions include the model, history and a name.

Saving and loading

  • Sessions will automatically save to disk.
  • Favorites show at the top of your session list for quick access.
  • Sessions are stored in the application's data directory.

Modifying Sessions

  • Right click on a session to open the modification dialog
  • You have the options to delete or rename sessions
  • Deleting a session will sync if enabled. IF sync is disabled and the deleted session is present on our servers, it will re-appear
  • Renaming a session will sync if enabled.

Export / Import

  • You can export sessions by signing in and enabling sync in settings.
  • Imported sessions are treated as local data and will automatically sync if enabled.
  • For security purposes, we require you to re-enable sync each time you sign in
